pmi_data_depth_w
2 to 131072
256
The device depth for the LUT based FIFO_DC range from 2 to 8192. The LatticeECP2/M, LatticeECP3, LatticeSC/M, and LatticeXP2 device family data depths range from 2 to 131072. The LatticeECP/EC and LatticeXP data depths range from 2 to 65536. The MachXO and Platform Manager data depths range from 2 to 16384. The LatticeSC/M, Platform Manager, and MachXO device families support different read/write depths and different read/write data widths.
