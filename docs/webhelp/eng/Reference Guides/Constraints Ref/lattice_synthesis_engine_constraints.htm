Lattice Synthesis Engine (LSE) Constraints

The Lattice Synthesis Engine (LSE) enables you to use constraints that are directly interpreted by the synthesis engine. This new category of constraints includes Synopsys Design Constraints (SDC), such as create_clock, and Lattice Synthesis Engine-Supported HDL Attributes such as syn_black_box. These constraints influence the optimization or structure of the output netlist.
