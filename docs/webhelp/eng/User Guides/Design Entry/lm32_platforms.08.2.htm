About LFMNX and Platform Firmware Resiliency

The LFMNX FPGA is designed to meet the enhanced security requirements of the National Institutes of Standards and Technology (NIST) SP 800-193 specification for Platform Firmware Resiliency (PFR). With LFMNX you can produce a “Root of Trust” (RoT) FPGA to insure that the firmware on a board does not become corrupted. For more information about the Lattice solution for NIST SP 800-193, see www.latticesemi.com/PFR.

Lattice Propel provides a PFR module to start your LFMNX design. The PFR module is a pre-configured and pre-verified hardened block.
