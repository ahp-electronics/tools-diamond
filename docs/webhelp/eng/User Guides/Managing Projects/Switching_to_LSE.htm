Switching to Lattice Synthesis Engine

When changing to Lattice Synthesis Engine (LSE) from another synthesis tool, there are a few things to do:

Consider creating a new design implementation. You can experiment with different settings without losing your previous work. See Working with Implementations.
Modify the Synopsys Design Constraint (.sdc) file into an .ldc or create a new .ldc file. See Lattice Synthesis Engine (LSE) Constraints and Applying Lattice Synthesis Engine Constraints.
Adjust the LSE strategy settings. See LSE Options and Optimizing LSE for Area and Speed.
Review the LSE coding tips and consider applying them to your design. See Coding Tips for Lattice Synthesis Engine (LSE).
Review the instructions for integrated synthesis. There are some differences with LSE. See Integrated Synthesis.
