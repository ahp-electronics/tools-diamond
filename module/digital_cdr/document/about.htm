Low-speed CDR

Devices Support: LatticeSCM

Overview: The LSCDR (Low Speed Clock and Data Recovery) logic takes a clock at approximately the same frequency as the incoming data, then produces this same data re-timed with the appropriate clock phase.

 

Features: 

·         Capable of retiming data from 155MHz to 400 MHz.

·         Can accommodate jitter components up to 0.25UI.

·         No additional logic required ( FIFOs built-in).

·         Can handle max. 18 channels of data.

Top Level IP:

The Low-speed CDR core, henceforth referred to as the IP core, is essentially made up of the following blocks

 

·         Phase generation block.

·         CDR (8 channels total).

·         Synchronous FIFO (one per channel).
